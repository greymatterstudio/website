Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Most clients see their first qualified calls within 60 days of launch. By month three, you should be consistently generating 10–30 sales calls per month from your YouTube channel alone.

Do I need a large existing audience to get started?

No. We build the system from scratch. Whether you have zero subscribers or 100,000, we start by positioning your channel to attract the right audience — not just any audience. However we only work with founders with a proven offer.

What does 'Done For You' actually mean?

Everything. We handle strategy, scripting, recording direction, editing, thumbnails, SEO, upload and scheduling. Your only job is to show up and record. We take care of the rest.

What niches do you specialise in?

We work with B2B service providers, coaches, consultants, property investors, accountants, health professionals, and more. If you sell a high-ticket service, we can build the system for you.

How is this different from hiring a video editor?

We're not a video editing service — we're a YouTube authority agency. We build an entire lead generation system around your channel. An editor gives you a finished video. We give you a pipeline of qualified sales calls.
